Privacy Policy for Qimam ul Qudrat

Last Updated: September 2025

At Qimam ul Qudrat, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our educational aptitude test application handles information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We respect your privacy. The app does not collect, store, or process any personal data such as:

  • Name, email address, or contact details
  • Location information
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage or tracking analytics
  • Payment details (all handled by Apple directly)

2. Test Data and AI Features

  • All test questions and answers are processed locally on your device.
  • AI features, if available, analyze responses locally without sending data to any server.
  • Your results and progress remain only on your device and are never accessible to us.

3. Subscriptions and Purchases

Our app offers optional premium features:

  • All purchases are processed securely through Apple’s App Store.
  • We never see or store your payment or billing information.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings.

4. Third-Party Services

The app relies only on Apple services for distribution and payments:

  • Apple App Store – app distribution and subscriptions.
  • Apple In-App Purchases – secure payments.

Please review Apple’s privacy practices here: Apple Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit your data, there is no risk of data breach from our servers. Your information stays safely on your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13 or from any users.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be shown in the app update notes and on this page.
