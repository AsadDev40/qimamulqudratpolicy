Privacy Policy for Qimam ul Qudrat

Last Updated: 2024

Welcome to Qimam ul Qudrat, an educational aptitude test application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Qimam ul Qudrat is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information including:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Payment information (all subscriptions are handled through Apple's secure payment system)

2. Test Data and AI Features

The app provides aptitude tests and questions for educational purposes:

  • All test questions and answers are processed locally on your device
  • Any AI-powered features analyze responses locally and do not transmit data to external servers
  • Your test results and progress are stored only on your device and are not accessible to us

3. Subscription Information

Qimam ul Qudrat offers optional in-app purchases for premium features:

  • All subscription payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment details or financial information
  • Subscription management is handled entirely through your Apple ID settings

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and subscription management
  • Apple's In-App Purchase system: For secure payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your information.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no risk of data breach from our servers. All your test progress and results remain securely on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Qimam ul Qudrat is an educational app suitable for users of all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app update notes and on this page.
